人生就是受苦

Feb 10, 2020 | 閱讀

I mean human existence is characterized by a fair bit of suffering. We’re limited creatures and life is very hard. Everyone dies, all of the things you do will eventually fail, suffering is a certainty. It’s very easy for people to become resentful about being, about existence. These kids who shoot up high schools, these mass shooters, they’re the perfect examples of people who run on nothing but resentment. That’s the dark side of the ideological possession: I get to decide who my enemies are, and I get to go after them for every single thing that’s ever been done to me that it isn’t good.

人類的存在可以說有一大部分都是在受苦，我們能力有限然後生存又非常艱難，每個人最後都是死，你做的所有事情最後也都會失敗，這種生活怎能不覺得艱難困苦。所以人們很容易對生活感到憤恨，覺得為什麼人生在世要一直受苦受難。那些學校槍擊案跟無差別屠殺的兇手們就是最好的例子，他們內心除了恨之外沒有其他的東西，也突顯了以意識形態認知世界的暗黑影響：世界非黑即白，只要誰曾經待我不好，他就是我的敵人，而我有權決定我要怎麼對付他。

Order is the place you are when you do works, and chaos is where you are when you do doesn’t work. And no matter where you go, order and chaos exist and your job is to be right on the border between those, the same place where these things stack up. A proper life is when these things stack up harmoniously.

秩序就是當你的行為可以達成你的目的，混亂要是你的行為無法達成你的目的。不管什麼時候，秩序混亂都是共存的，你的工作就是將兩者做一個平衡。當你趨近了平衡，那就是我所定義的，一個人該有的生活。

Life is suffering, and suffering can make people resentful, murderous and genocidal if you take it far enough. So people need an antidote to suffering: some would build up walls of luxury around and think that’ll protect them from suffering; some could build a delusion and live inside that, but these eventually are gonna fall apart. What really helps you fight against suffering is the truth. Truth puts reality behind you. So with truth in mind, you are able to face the reality that’s coming straight at you without becoming weak and degenerating and resentful and wishing for the destruction of being.

生活就是受苦，而受苦會讓人憤恨，想殺人，講得極端一點，甚至會讓人想要殲滅全人類。所以我們需要一個解毒劑來面對這麼悲催的人生，有人用財富，有人用不切實際的樂觀，不過這兩個都沒辦法長久。唯一我認為能夠抵抗受苦的，就是說實話，以認識真實為所有行動的方針。如果你以了解真實做為生活的心態，當現實往你直撲而來，你不會被幻象所騙，可以好好的直視現實，進而有機會克服現實，也就不會因為幻想破滅而感到憤恨想毀滅世界。
